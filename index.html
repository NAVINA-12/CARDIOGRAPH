What is Cardiograph?

Medical specialty dealing with the diagnosis and treatment of diseases and abnormalities involving the heart and blood vessels

Why monitor blood pressure (BP)?

Hypertension (high blood pressure) is an important health issue in children, because of its association with obesity. High blood pressure is considered a risk factor for heart disease and stroke, and high BP in childhood has been linked to high BP in adulthood.

Your Pulse In Our Vision

PQRST Wave

  1. P-Wave:It’s the initial upward deflection on the ECG.
  2. PR Interval:It reflects the time taken for the electrical impulse to travel from the atria to the ventricles.
  3. QRS Complex:The QRS complex signifies ventricular depolarization
  4. ST Segment:It should be isoelectric (at the baseline) unless there’s an underlying issue.
  5. T-Wave:The T-wave reflects ventricular repolarization.
